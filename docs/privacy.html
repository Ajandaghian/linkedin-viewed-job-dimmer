LinkedIn Viewed Job Dimmer and Highlighter Privacy Policy

LinkedIn Viewed Job Dimmer is designed to run locally in the browser.

What it collects

  • No personal data is collected or sent to a server
  • No LinkedIn content is uploaded anywhere
  • The only stored values are the user-selected run mode and keyword groups for highlighting

How storage is used

  • The extension stores its mode and keyword groups locally in Chrome extension storage
  • These settings are used only to restore your preferences the next time you open the popup

Third parties

  • LinkedIn Viewed Job Dimmer does not share data with third parties

If you have questions about this extension, contact the publisher through the Chrome Web Store listing or repository.
